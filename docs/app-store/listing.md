## Description

> Thinking Errors NotePad is a quiet place to untangle a stressful moment,
> the way you would on a paper CBT worksheet — but built for your phone.
>
> WRITE IT OUT
> Describe the situation, name how it felt, then free-write everything going
> through your head. No prompts interrupting you, no character limits.
>
> HIGHLIGHT THE THOUGHTS
> Sweep your finger over the sentences that sting — like running a highlighter
> across the page.
>
> NAME THE THINKING ERRORS
> Label each highlighted thought with common thinking errors (cognitive
> distortions): all-or-nothing thinking, catastrophizing, mind reading,
> fortune telling, and more.
>
> FIND A MORE BALANCED THOUGHT
> Re-read what you wrote with the labels in view, then write the calmer,
> fairer version.
>
> PRIVATE BY ARCHITECTURE
> • No account, no sign-in, no server — the app never makes a network request
> • Everything you write stays on your device
> • No analytics, no ads, no AI reading your words
> • Export a clean printable copy to keep or share with your therapist,
>   or a JSON backup of everything
>
> AN HONEST NOTE ABOUT YOUR DATA
> Because entries live only on your device, deleting the app deletes them.
> Export regularly — this is a working notepad, not long-term storage.
>
> Thinking Errors NotePad is free and open source. It is a self-help writing
> tool, not therapy, medical advice, or a medical device. Many therapists use
> thought records as homework between sessions — bring your exports along.
