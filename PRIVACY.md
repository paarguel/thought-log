# Privacy Policy — Thought Record

*Last updated: July 8, 2026*

Thought Record is built so that we **cannot** see your data, because
your data never reaches us.

## What we collect

Nothing.

- **No account.** There is no sign-up, sign-in, or user identifier.
- **No server.** The app has no backend. It never makes a network request.
- **No analytics or tracking.** No usage data, no crash reporters, no ads,
  no third-party SDKs that phone home.

## Where your writing lives

Everything you write is stored **only on your device**, in the app's local
storage. Nobody — including the developer — can read it.

- **Drafts** autosave on the device while you work.
- **Saved entries** stay on the device until you delete them.
- **Exports** (printable HTML, JSON, or the full backup file) are files you
  create and control. Once you export a file, where it goes is up to you —
  for example, a file you save to a synced folder is governed by that
  service's policies.

## Data loss — please read

Because entries exist only on your device, **deleting the app, losing the
device, or the operating system clearing storage permanently deletes your
entries.** There is no recovery, because no copy exists anywhere else. Use
**Your data → Export all entries** regularly if you want to keep your
writing. The app is a working notepad, not long-term storage.

## Not medical care

Thought Record is a self-help writing tool inspired by CBT-style
thought records. It is not therapy, medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment,
and it is not a medical device. If you are in crisis or need support, contact
a licensed professional or your local emergency services.

## Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If a future version ever changes this posture (it is not planned), the policy
will be updated here and the change will be called out in the app before
anything is sent anywhere.
